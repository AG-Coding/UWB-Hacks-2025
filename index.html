About BioTrack

BioTrack is an innovative platform focused on the seamless, efficient, and secure transportation of biological materials. Built to empower research institutions, hospitals, and biotech companies, our system ensures the integrity and traceability of every shipment, from pickup to delivery.

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

GPS-enabled tracking and condition monitoring (temperature, humidity, shock detection).

Data Integrity

Blockchain-secured logs to ensure tamper-proof tracking records.

AI Optimization

Smart route planning using machine learning to reduce transit time and risks.

Compliance & Security

Designed to meet strict healthcare and biotech logistics regulations globally.
